What can I do for you?

Below are the services I offer professionally — either independently or in collaboration with trusted expert partners:

✔ Accessibility Testing and Remediation

  • Testing the accessibility of your website, mobile app, or document (PDF, Word, etc.).
  • Identifying and fixing accessibility barriers in compliance with WCAG 2.2 (W3C), and aligning with European, American, and Macedonian laws: the Web Accessibility Directive (EU) 2016/2102, the European Accessibility Act (EU) 2019/882, the U.S. Rehabilitation Act (Section 508), and North Macedonia’s national Law on Accessibility of Websites and Mobile Apps in the Public Sector.
  • Creating evaluations and reports on the current accessibility status of a website, app, or digital document; providing recommendations for improvements; and preparing accessibility statements.

✔ Accessibility and Assistive Technology Consulting & Training

  • Training and mentoring for individuals and organizations on specific assistive technologies (software or hardware).
  • Workshops on creating accessible content and using accessibility tools.
  • Demonstrations and presentations with screen readers and other technologies.

✔ Macedonian Language Services

  • Translation of user interfaces, apps, and websites into Macedonian.
  • Proofreading and language editing of Macedonian content.
  • Writing original Macedonian content on a wide range of topics.

Each service can be provided individually or as a bundle depending on your needs. Whether you’re an individual, NGO, company, or institution — I’m here to collaborate.

Feel free to contact me if you have a project or question in mind!
