Who am I?

I am a certified accessibility specialist, independent researcher, and Macedonian language proofreader, passionate about accessibility, inclusion, and solving real-world problems through technology and language.

With over five years of professional experience, my journey includes:

  • Certification in Accessibility Core Competencies by the International Association of Accessibility Professionals (IAAP).
  • Certified NVDA screen reader expert and trainer.
  • Member and national contact for North Macedonia in the Association for the Advancement of Assistive Technology in Europe (AAATE).
  • Bachelor’s degree in Macedonian Language and Literature from the Faculty of Philology “Blaze Koneski” in Skopje, with maximum GPA.
  • Licensed proofreader for Macedonian language.
  • Currently enrolled in the master’s program for Project Management at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Information Technologies (FEIT) in Skopje.

Beyond professional qualifications, I am someone who:

  • Uses assistive technology daily as a blind individual—making accessibility not just a job, but a life tool.
  • Understands the lived experience of people with disabilities, not just through work but through shared experience and community engagement.
  • Is determined, persistent, and driven by the desire to help others live, work, and communicate with ease and dignity.
